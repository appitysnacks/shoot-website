The Kagency

Karrie Goldberg, President & Founder of The Kagency

Karrie Goldberg is the President and Founder of The Kagency, a New York City based entertainment agency behind a slew of the most premiere events and parties across New York, Chicago, Miami and Los Angeles. Representing both a global roster of talent and leading-edge event spaces and venues, The Kagency is the connection to the best people and places.

The Kagency boasts an equally impressive Client roster including companies spanning all industries. American Express, Unilever Volkswagon to W Hotels, Yves Saint Laurent and Vogue.

Q: Thanks for talking to us today. How did you come to found The Kagency?

The Kagency (AKA Kage Konsulting) truly happened organically, it is a culmination of my interests, experience in hospitality and extensive database. First, we started by agenting for locations that had a primary source of business, like nightclubs and photography studios, as their in-house event marketing and booking team , then we began to take on full-time event spaces, real estate /retail clients, hotels and more. Next, our clients began to ask us to source talent, mainly Djs, for events; so we started to represent corporate friendly artists. Some of our other verticals are out of home media buying tied to our event clients and locations in addition to general consulting on location marketing and sales for hospitality clients. As my clients’ requests evolved, so did the business.


Q: I know that music is a big part of The Kagency, how did you come to develop such a roster of incredible music talent?

I have always been a bit of a DJ junkie, starting way back when I was clubbing in Chicago in my teens. Through working with amazing venues like Cielo for 10 years, where I not only got to know some of my all-time favorite producers and Djs, but really fine-tune my understanding of talent marketing and booking and then onto our massive event production venues where I gained an invaluable understanding of what talent is needed for corporate clients, the music side of the business has a very diverse portfolio. That being said, even though I am a house music girl at heart, I love listening to 90s hip hop, lots of 80s New Wave, Reggae and a bit of funk too! I have been very fortunate to work or on events with some of my favorite groups like Duran Duran, Depeche Mode, Prince, Ziggy Marley and more!


Q: So I know that The Kagency is your main focus now but I know that you’ve done some other things earlier on in your career that are a little different, can you tell me about that.

Ha yes, well..many are surprised to know that I majored in accounting with a double minor in French & Japanese, which led me to working at PriceWaterhouseCoopers in Japan! I had done a semester abroad at Sophia University in Tokyo and fell in love with the culture and became hyper focused on becoming more fluent. I had the most amazing experience living and working there and made some incredible life-long friends.


Q: How has Shoot helped with running your business?

I absolutely love Shoot. I travel a lot internationally for my business and that usually means I have several phone numbers and local contact details in play at any given time. Instead of scribbling things down, I keep all my info in Shoot and just set up additional profiles with the info I want to share on the fly. Shoot has become a huge time saver and helps me get connected fast wherever I am.

My business is really about making connections and brining the best people together to create the best events and experiences. I’m constantly connecting people and making introductions so the ‘Share a Friend’s Info’ feature lets me share info from my own contacts list so quickly and easily – anything that can makes doing simple but often time-consuming tasks faster and more convenient is a keeper in my book.
