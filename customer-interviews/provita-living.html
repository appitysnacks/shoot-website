Provita Living

Danielle Karuna, Managing Director & Co-Founder of Provita Living

A graduate of NYU’s Tisch School of the Arts and certified Jivamukti Yoga Instructror and Lululemon Ambasador, Danielle Karuna (in partnership with her Husband Kris) is Co-founder of Provita Living, an on-demand wellness company focused on bringing fitness experiences to their customers when and where they need them to be. With just over a year under its belt, Provita is already expanding its New York based operations and brining Provita to Los Angeles this year.

Q: How did Provita get started?

My husband Kris and I founded Provita just over a year ago now. It was really something that evolved organically based on a direction we both saw ourselves going in. As a yoga teacher for many years, I’ve seen and experienced myself how yoga and being active in general can help transform one’s life. In NYC there are so many people visiting for so many reasons and being away from their regular fitness routines with little or no time to look for something to do, we saw there was an opportunity to bring fitness to them. That was the original idea behind Provita, we wanted to provide fitness on-demand, kind of like an Uber for fitness. We have easy online booking and have partnered with several hotels across the city as their premier provider for on-demand fitness.


Q: What types of fitness programs do you offer?

We have an across-the-board offering – Our yoga teachers have specialties in a range of styles including Ashtanga and Jivamukti to the popular power and vinyasa yoga styles. We also offer personal fitness training and our team of certified trainers have expertise ranging from cross-training and conditioning to boot-camp styles as well as kick boxing. Everything we offer is based on when our Clients need us – we’ve done a yoga class at midnight for a client about to head to Hong Kong on a redeye, a mini-marathon tour of Manhattan at 6am for a group of engineers visiting from the UK for a conference and most recently we filled in for a Pilates session with very well-known actress when her teacher failed to show. Now that was cool!


Q: I know things have gotten really busy for Provita this year – can you tell us a bit about that?

Yes it’s been a whirlwind! Ever since we got started the feedback has been incredibly positive. We’ve expanded our service offerings beyond on-demand fitness services to include customized promotional events and retreats for our partner hotels in NYC as well as hosting our own Provita-inspired events.

We’ve also just recently expanded to Los Angeles where we’re in the process of relocating our Headquarters.


Q: Can you tell us how Shoot has helped you with your Business?

Running my own business is something I’ve always wanted to do and it’s really helped me prioritize what’s most important. One of the things I realized was that I was spending a lot of unnecessary time finding ways to give people information about the company, our upcoming event schedule and my schedule of classes. While we use our website and social media channels much as any other small business would, I felt like I was losing opportunities to connect with people in a meaningful way in the moment. Shoot has given me a way to do this by helping me organize all my information all in one place so I can share my info with anyone in just a couple taps.
