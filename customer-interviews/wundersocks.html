Wundersocks

Lukas and Martin Mairl, Co-Founders of Wundersocks

Lukas and Martin Mairl are brothers and Co-Founders of Wundersocks, an Italian sportswear company that has developed a line of high performance socks that meet the demands of world-class athletes as well as those with equally high demands for comfort, style and luxury.

Q: How did Wundersocks get started?

For over 30 years our family has run the largest retail sporting goods store in the Tyrolean Alps in Northern Italy where we’re from. Pretty much all year round it’s a hot spot for tourists and outdoor enthusiasts who come to ski, snowboard, hike or just take in the breathtaking views of the Alps. Since almost all outdoor activities here require some kind of footwear, we hear firsthand from our customers about the problems they might be having with their gear. The most common ones are usually related to the foot and ones you might experience doing almost any kind of sport - blisters, friction and of course, sweaty feet.

What most people don’t know is that a great majority of foot problems are not actually caused by footwear itself but by wearing the wrong socks. Because socks provide the cushion between your foot and your shoe, they can either prevent or cause the friction that eventually leads to irritation and injury. If socks are too tight, too thick, too loose, rough in texture, or made of the wrong material, that friction multiplies and so does the risk of developing problems. We saw that there was a lot of room for improvement in the area of athletic socks and with so many people coming to us every day with the same problems, we thought it was a great opportunity to see what we could do to help.

After a couple of years and several hundred prototypes later, we’ve developed a sock that alleviates the most common ailments that is equally functional, comfortable, and stylish. Our socks are made from a special blend of merino wool that combines its natural and known beneficial properties with our ‘no-slouch’ knit that keeps socks in place so you’re not left with those terrible elastic marks when you take them off. They’re also incredibly luxurious so that you’ll want to wear them all the time, even when you’re just lounging around at home. And of course, all of our socks are made right here in Italy.


Q: How did you come up with the name, Wundersocks?

When we first started out we were so focused on research and development that we didn’t even think to have a name. Being based in northern Italy, many of our customers come from neighboring countries like Germany and Austria and when they’d visit our store they would ask for the ‘Wundersocks’. Even now we have customers driving for sometimes over two hours one-way just to buy a pair of ‘Wundersocks’. We’d like to think of ourselves as a customer driven company so we thought why not? the name just stuck.


Q: What are the numbers sewn into the toe area of the socks for?

My brother and I both love extreme sports and we’ve been skiing since we were kids but it’s always important to remember to be safe, especially when mountains, snow and ice are involved. While the emergency number ‘911’ is known the world over thanks to Hollywood, it’s a known fact here that only a quarter of EU citizens actually know their local emergency number. Across Europe you can call ‘112’ and in Italy ‘118’ and we sew both of these numbers into the toe of each sock. The idea here was not for people to remove their boots in case of an emergency but for it to act as a friendly reminder every time you put on your socks to always be safe and look out for each other.


Q: How many styles do you have now and where can you buy Wundersocks?

We currently have about seven different styles to suit a range of different activities – running, golfing, hiking, skiing, snowboarding and of course styles for everyday wear. We also have sizes specifically designed for kids.

We currently have four retail stores, three in South Tyrol and the fourth in Innsbruck, Austria. We also ship worldwide via our online store.


Q: How has Shoot helped you with running your business day-to-day?

We’ve developed a strong niche following but our ultimate goal is to make Wundersocks available and accessible to everyone in the world. We attend several large tradeshows throughout the year where we meet with potential new distributors and partners. Shoot has become an invaluable tool in these situations and has given me a way to create more opportunities to tell people about Wundersocks and give them all the information they would need to get in touch with us to learn more about our products.

I also have a profile setup for each store location so if I meet someone while travelling I can quickly email or text them the information for the store closest to them. Of course I can always just tell them our website URL but if they have the info in an email they don’t have to worry about forgetting the information later. We also have a growing sales team covering different regions across Europe and they’re all now using Shoot as well.
