Magnet Strategy Group

Dani Peters, President & Founder of Magnet Strategy Group

Dani Peters is the President and Founder of Magnet Strategy Group, a Toronto and Washington, D.C.-based government relations practice focused on creating opportunities for companies with the U.S. and Canadian government.

Q: Thanks for speaking with us today Dani. Could you tell us a bit about your background and how Magnet Strategy Group got started?

Great to be here! After finishing my undergraduate degree in Political Science I decided to pursue a Masters degree in Commercial Diplomacy and I think that’s where I really began to hone my interest in public affairs and policy development. After graduating I worked for over a decade with companies across various sectors on both sides of the boarder to help them connect with the right public groups and develop the most effective public affairs strategies. I’ve always wanted to have my own firm so when the time felt right I decided to venture out on my own and founded Magnet Strategy Group in 2014.


Q: Can you tell us a bit more about what Magnet does?

Sure, we’re basically the people you’ll find at the intersection of businesses and government – we act as advocates on behalf of our Clients by becoming the go-between between them and the government groups related to their industry. Government decision-making is full of twists and turns so a major part of what we do is function as the communicator and navigator to make sure that the right people on both sides are hearing the key issues. At the end of the day we’re really looking to find the synergies between the private and public sectors to advance and promote the development of public policies that benefit everyone.


Q: Can you describe what a typical day is like for you?

Well I have 3 kids so this mean I’m up at the crack of dawn…or earlier! While my husband and I get them ready for school I’m usually checking emails and responding to anything urgent that might have come up over night. After the kids are organized and off to school I’ll head to the office and meet with my Team and prepare for any meetings or presentations we have that day. Afternoons are usually spent on conference calls with our team based in D.C., reviewing briefs for upcoming projects as well as making sure my staff here in Toronto has everything they need. Of course, some of this changes when I’m travelling but it’s basically the same thing, just turbo charged!


Q: How do you use Shoot in your day to day and how has it helped?

I love Shoot! As a business owner, my priorities are always on making sure my Clients are getting the best service possible and on new business opportunities. I attend a lot of conferences and there’s definitely a trend of people preferring digital information to paper business cards. Shoot really helps me share my info digitally with people I meet on the spot - I can connect with them immediately and because it’s all digital, I have an instant record of all the people I met that day.
