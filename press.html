What is Shoot Contact Sharing?

Every person we meet presents a new opportunity. We humans (most of us anyways), still need other humans in order to succeed on this earth. Whether it’s a customer who buys a product we make or someone using a service we provide, the things we do each day have little value unless they reach other people in some shape, way or form. We created Shoot Contact Sharing as a tool to help people take advantage of the brief moments when meeting someone new so that they never miss another opportunity to connect in an actionable way.

Shoot Contact Sharing makes it easy to keep your contact information organized and so that you can easily create profiles using any combination of your info for all the different situations and scenarios you might find yourself in. There are multiple way to share (or 'Shoot' as we call it) a profile to someone you meet - via email, SMS text message and via messaging apps if it's someone you're already connected with.

The profile info you share is delivered in simple text format (or as a vCard file if you select that option) which makes it universally acceptable, readable and searchable by the recipient. The best thing about Shoot Contact Sharing is that you can use it with anyone, the other person doesn't need to have the app in order for you to use it.


Who is using Shoot Contact Sharing?

Anyone with an iOS or Android smartphone can use Shoot Contact Sharing. These are a few of examples of people and purposes Shoot can help:
